Long Story Short

Muriquee is the online extension of a growing and vibrant community of artists and event organizers that started years ago in Berlin. From an abbandoned train repair wharehouse to the old roof of a beer faktory the community evolved till its current location at Loophole Berlin.

With Muriquee you will be able to build up your tour and create events, visually.

If you want to get more involved write us, or pass by in person when you are in Berlin,
